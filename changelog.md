# Modified model files

modifications © VTT Technical Research Centre of Finland Ltd 2023-2026 CC BY 4.0

- SysSettings: Comms, timeperiods, interpolation.
- VT_EUReg_AGR_V2p0: Added biofuel blending options for agriculture fuels.
- VT_EUReg_COM_V2p0: Added biofuel and P2X blending options for oil and gas.
- VT_EUReg_ELC_V2p0: Dist heat fuel shares modified for FI.
- VT_EUReg_ELC_V2p0: Chemical pulp tech modified for FI.
- VT_EUReg_RSD_V2p0: Added biofuel and P2X blending options for oil and gas.
- VT_EUReg_SUP_V2p0: Recycled paper mining updated for FI. 

- SubRES_00_Nuc-EU: Updates to several nuclear power plant start years, lifetimes .
- SubRES_00_Nuc-EU_Trans: Fi-Olkiluoko NCAP_BND.
- SubRES_10_TECHS_CHP_SUP_IND: Bio-CCS techs added; updates to dist heating INVCOST, EFF, AFA; CO2 storage procs updated.
- SubRES_11_TECHS_Power: Extend bio-CCS lifetimes, postpone unknown nuclear start year.
- SubRES_15_TECHS_HYDROGEN: Hydrogen and CCS techs updated.
- SubRES_20_TECHS_TRA: Electric transport techs added.
- SubRES_20_TECHS_TRA_Trans: Updates to EFF and AFA.
- SubRES_21_TECHS_OTHER: Simplified natural gas storage flows to GASNAT only.
- SubRES_22_TECHS_BIOBEFINERIES: RFNBO blend processes added, renewable fuel blending up and lo bounds.
- SubRES_23_TECHS_CCUS: Split processes into atm+bio-co2 and fossil-co2. added CCUS techs new DAC, EW, SCS, SOC.
- SubRES_30_TECHS_HeatingCooling: RSD-ELC-split added, heat pump techs bug fixes.
- SubRES_30_TECHS_HeatingCooling_Trans: heat pump techs bug fixes.

- Scen_20_Demand_BAU2025: Updated TRA demands; updated IPH and IPL demands for FI.
- Scen_BAU-General: End GASNAT import from RU.
- Scen_BIOPOT_BAU2025: New BAU limits to biofeedstock, bioimports modified.
- Scen_BIOPOT_EU2040S3: New BAU limits to biofeedstock, based on EU 2040 impact assessment.
- Scen_CO2_100: Minor mods to TOTCO2 bound.
- Scen_CoalPhaseOut: Modified from KillCoal-original file.
- Scen_Emis_CO2: Updates to CO2 factors, added FOSCO2 comm.
- Scen_ENUC: Merged nuclear scens into one file. Updates to FI and IT nuc.
- Scen_FuelPrices_BAU2025: Updated fossil fuel prices.
- Scen_TRA_EVPHEV_NewSalesHigh: Update CO2 limits for new vehicles, higher end for HDT.
- Scen_TRA_EVPHEV_NewSalesBase: Update CO2 limits for new vehicles, lower end for HDT.
- Scen_TRA_SCENARIOS: Disabled diesel NCAP_BNDs-
- Scen_UC_TRA: Disable lower limit for gasolene cars. 
- Scen_ZATEMP_Calibration_V2: Heating projections update.
- Scen_X-NATIONALGRIDS: Natural gas grid updates.
- ScenTrade_02_TradeParameters: Added trade for fossil CO2 capture.
